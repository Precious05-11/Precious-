Knitting

My favorite hobby

Knitting is the process of interlooping yarn to create textile fabric made of interconnected loops, done by hand, machine or both. Knitting creates stitches: loops of yarn in a row; the loops are created with a pair of knitting needles, which can either be straight and cylindrical or in the round with plastic tubes or wire (cables) joining the ends of both.
